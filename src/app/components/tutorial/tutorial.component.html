How to Play

Peeple vs. Sheeple is all about choice. You choose who you want to play as, their name, and which of two scenarios you/they prefer. Will you end up as an individual that thinks like the peeple, or just one of the herd of sheeple?

Be watched once while you sleep, but you know when/where.

OR

Be watched while you sleep a total of ten times, but you never know when/where.
